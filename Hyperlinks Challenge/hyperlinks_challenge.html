The beginning life of a coder, so they say...

Hi, I'm Sam (for short, that is). This will be my second week into the Code Space programme this coming Friday.
So far the experience has been GREAT.

From what I am use to or considered to be my "comfort zone", this course by far is something completely different
it's definitely not something I saw myself doing, but deciding to take that leap of stepping out of my little zone
and discovering that coding - web development is so much fun and interesting and literally something you constantly
have to update yourself and knowledge on.

My introduction to my first module, HTML, what an exciting way to start off something completely new to myself,
learning about how to construct a website, how to code a table and nesting a table (a table within a table).
You get taught forms and the different uses for it, also writing an article out on a website.
I'm honestly excited to learn more and build on a new skill.

The course is fast-paced since we have to get a lot in within a short period of time, that's why time management
would play a big role in how you construct yourself, but if you manage to complete all projects on time and hand in
challenges you're done, you should'nt have a problem.
